Live The Adventure

Located near a prime location in the Canadian rockies, Camp Eden is your own little slice of heaven. Far away from the hustling and bustling day to day of life, take a break and getaway to our little paradise. Gaze in awe at the beautiful vistas and backdrops of the white snowy mountains. the many rich lakes surrounding the campsite,the many wonderful rich wildlife in their natural habitat and listen to the sounds of nature. If your lucky you may bear witness to the magnificent aurouras at night. This truly is heaven its tranquil and vibrant. Take part in a large assortment of activities, take a hike up our long trail and discover new wonders and magnificent vantage points. Take a ddip in the surrounding oceans and rivers or go kayaking down the river pass. Mountain biking is another activity we offer so you can explore our setting at breakneck speed. Or simply relax at your leisure near a campfire and mingle with friends and family, whatever you do its sure to be an adventure!
